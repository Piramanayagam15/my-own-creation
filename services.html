Our Bridal Services

A complete range of bridal beauty and design services to make every ceremony unforgettable.

Bridal Makeup

From subtle, natural glam to full traditional bridal looks, our makeup is customized for your skin tone, features, and outfit. We use high-quality, long-wear products so you look fresh in person and flawless in photos.

  • HD, airbrush, and traditional bridal makeup options
  • Pre-bridal consultation to understand your style
  • Separate looks for muhurtham, reception, and events
  • Skin-prep for a smooth, radiant finish

Mehndi (Henna)

Beautifully detailed mehndi designs that tell your story through patterns and motifs. We plan designs to suit your taste, outfit, and cultural preferences.

  • Full bridal mehndi for hands and feet
  • Custom designs with traditional and modern elements
  • Guest mehndi packages for friends and family
  • Deep, long-lasting stain using quality henna

Aari Embroidery

Elevate your bridal outfit with rich aari work that complements your jewellery and enhances your saree or lehenga.

  • Custom aari designs for bridal blouses and lehengas
  • Motifs inspired by temple, floral, and contemporary art
  • Bead, stone, and zari detailing for a luxurious finish
  • Designs planned to match your wedding theme

Blouse Designing

Perfectly fitted, beautifully designed blouses that enhance your silhouette and complete your bridal look.

  • Neck and sleeve patterns tailored to your style
  • Blouses for muhurtham, reception, and functions
  • Coordinated designs with aari, stone, and cutwork
  • Focus on comfort so you can move with ease

Hair Styling & Draping

Elegant hairstyles and secure saree/dupatta draping that stays in place through all your rituals, photographs, and celebrations.

  • Traditional bun, braid, and floral hairstyles
  • Modern and fusion looks for sangeet and reception
  • Professional saree and dupatta draping
  • Use of hair accessories to balance your jewellery

Training & Tutorials

Learn the art of bridal beauty with structured training programs designed for beginners and intermediate learners.

  • Bridal makeup basics and advanced techniques
  • Mehndi design fundamentals and bridal patterns
  • Aari work stitches, motifs, and finishing methods
  • Business tips to start your own bridal studio

Training batches are limited to small groups for personal attention. Contact us for current batch dates and fees.
